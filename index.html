Easy Setup

Start the app. One click to create and deploy your blog. That easy.

All the post content is written in Markdown. This means you can use any text editor to write your posts. Or, our built-in editor.

Rust-Powered

Written in Rust, a fast and efficient programming language for a smooth user experience.

Open Source

Fully open source. Contribute, customize, and make it your own. Create your own blog templates, too!

Desktop App

A sleek, native desktop application for a smooth writing experience. No web-app shenanigans.

Custom Templates

Create your own templates or choose from our store. Your blog, your style.

WYSIWYG Editor

What you see is what you get. Edit your blog visually with our intuitive editor.

One-Click Deploy

Deploy your site to the web with a single click. No technical knowledge required.

Bring your own domain

Use your own domain or use one from a supported provider (currently Netlify).
